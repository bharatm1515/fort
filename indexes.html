Popular forts near Pune

  1. Raigad Fort

    It stands on Sahyadri mountain range in Mahad city.

    It was build by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in 14th century. Raigad fort is well fortified.

    There are approximately 1737 steps leading to the fort.There also exist ropeway and it takes only 4 minutes

  2. Sinhgad Fort

    Sinhagad is a hill fortress located at around 35 km southwest of the city of Pune, India.

    Visiting Hours:5.00 am to 9.00 pm

  3. Pratapgad Fort

    Pratapgad fort is located 23 kilometres (14 mi) west of Mahabaleshwar, a popular hill station in the area.

    Visiting Hours:6.00 am to 8.00 pm

  4. Torna Fort

    Torna Fort is also known as Prachandagad and is a large fort located in Pune district,Maharashtra.

    A Menghai Devi temple is also referred to as the Tornaji temple and it is situated near the entrance of the fort.

  5. Purandar Fort

    The fort of Purandhar stands at 4,472 ft (1,387 m) above the sea level in the Western Ghats, 50 km to the southeast of Pune.

    The Purandar Fort is a popular tourist destination

    Visiting Hours:9.00 am to 5.00 pm
